HEALTHCARE FACILITY SPECIFICATION
Project: Cedar Valley Community Hospital — 2-Story Acute Care Hospital
Architect: HKS Healthcare Architecture
Date: July 2024

SITE & FOUNDATION
Building footprint: 200 ft x 150 ft (30,000 sq ft per floor, 60,000 sq ft total). Reinforced concrete mat foundation, 24-inch thick. Grade beams between columns. Basement mechanical chase (partial, 80 ft x 40 ft) under surgical wing. Building oriented with ED entrance on south, main entrance on east.

STRUCTURAL SYSTEM
Reinforced concrete frame with post-tensioned flat slab floors. Columns 24 in x 24 in at 30 ft bays. Floor-to-floor height: 14 ft (12 ft clear). Lateral system: concrete shear walls at elevator/stair cores. Roof: concrete slab with built-up roofing. Designed for Essential Facility (Risk Category IV).

VERTICAL CIRCULATION
- Elevator 1 (Patient): 5,000 lb, 6 ft x 8 ft cab, stretcher-capable
- Elevator 2 (Service): 5,000 lb, 6 ft x 8 ft cab
- Stairwell A (NE): 8 ft x 10 ft, concrete, pressurized
- Stairwell B (SW): 8 ft x 10 ft, concrete, pressurized

EXTERIOR ENVELOPE
- Walls: brick/precast panels, R-30 continuous insulation, air barrier
- Curtain wall: main lobby and ED entrance, insulated low-e glass
- Windows: aluminum, insulated, limited operable (patient rooms only)
- Roof: fully-adhered TPO, R-40 insulation, fall protection anchors
- Helipad: future provision, structural reinforcement in place

MECHANICAL / HVAC
- AHU-1 (Surgical): 18,000 CFM, 100% outdoor air capability
  - HEPA final filtration, energy recovery, redundant fans
  - OR suites: 20 ACH minimum, positive pressure
- AHU-2 (Patient Wing): 14,000 CFM
  - MERV-14 + MERV-17 filtration, 6 ACH minimum in patient rooms
  - Isolation rooms: 12 ACH, negative pressure, HEPA exhaust
- AHU-3 (Emergency): 8,000 CFM, 100% outdoor air for trauma
- Boiler plant: 2 x 2,000 MBH redundant condensing boilers
- Chiller plant: 2 x 150-ton centrifugal chillers, N+1 redundancy
- Medical gas: bulk O2, medical air compressor, vacuum pumps, N2O manifold
- Pneumatic tube system: 12 stations, 2 transfer units

ELECTRICAL
- Service: 480/277V, 3-phase, 3000A, dual utility feeds
- Emergency power: 500kW diesel generator (Essential Electrical System)
  - Life Safety branch, Critical branch, Equipment branch per NFPA 99
  - 10-second transfer for life safety
- UPS: 100kVA for OR lights, monitoring, IT
- Nurse call: IP-based, pillow speaker, staff locator badges
- Security: card access all doors, 64 cameras, infant/patient tracking

FIRE PROTECTION
- Fully sprinklered, wet system
- Smoke compartments per NFPA 101 Healthcare
- Fire alarm: voice evacuation, smoke detection all spaces
- OR: wet chemical suppression (not clean agent, per hospital preference)
- Kitchen: wet chemical hood suppression

PLUMBING
- Domestic hot water: 2 x 100-gallon commercial gas, recirculation loop
- Tempered water to patient fixtures (max 110°F)
- Acid waste system in laboratory
- Grease interceptor for kitchen
- Medical gas: piped O2, vacuum, medical air, N2O to all clinical spaces
