RESIDENTIAL BUILDING SPECIFICATION
Project: Single-Family Dwelling — 742 Evergreen Terrace
Architect: Morrison & Associates
Date: October 2024

SITE & FOUNDATION
The lot measures 60 ft wide by 120 ft deep. The house footprint is approximately 40 ft x 30 ft (1,200 sq ft per floor). Foundation is poured concrete slab-on-grade, 4 inches thick with 6-mil vapor barrier underneath. Perimeter footings are 24 inches deep x 16 inches wide. Foundation walls extend 8 inches above grade.

STRUCTURAL FRAMING
Two-story wood frame construction. Exterior walls are 2x6 studs at 16 inches on center. Interior walls are 2x4 studs at 16 inches on center. Floor-to-ceiling height is 9 ft on the first floor and 8 ft on the second floor. Roof is gable style with 6:12 pitch, engineered trusses at 24 inches on center. Ridge height is approximately 26 ft from grade.
