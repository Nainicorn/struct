COMMERCIAL OFFICE BUILDING SPECIFICATION
Project: Meridian Business Center — 2-Story Office with Basement Parking
Architect: Hartfield Design Group
Date: September 2024

SITE & FOUNDATION
The building occupies a rectangular footprint of 80 ft x 60 ft (4,800 sq ft per floor). The basement parking level extends the full footprint plus 10 ft on each side (100 ft x 80 ft = 8,000 sq ft). Foundation consists of reinforced concrete spread footings with 12-inch thick basement walls. Basement slab is 6 inches thick with waterproof membrane.

STRUCTURAL SYSTEM
Steel frame construction with composite metal deck floors. Columns are W12x72 at 20 ft bays in both directions. Floor-to-floor height is 14 ft (11 ft clear ceiling on office floors). Roof is flat with steel joists at 5 ft on center and 1.5-inch metal deck. Basement ceiling height is 10 ft clear.

BASEMENT LEVEL — PARKING GARAGE
- Open parking area: 100 ft x 80 ft, 8,000 sq ft
- 45 parking spaces total (9 ft x 18 ft each including drive aisles)
- Two-way drive aisle: 24 ft wide, running east-west
- Concrete slab floor with sealed surface
- Two stairwells: northwest and southeast corners (6 ft x 10 ft each)
- One elevator shaft: center north (6 ft x 8 ft)
- Mechanical/electrical room: 15 ft x 20 ft, southwest corner
- Fire riser room: 6 ft x 8 ft, adjacent to mechanical room
- Entry ramp: 12 ft wide, 15% slope, south side

DOORS
- Main entry: 72-inch aluminum storefront double doors with panic hardware

- Parking entry: 36-inch steel fire-rated (90 min)
- Interior offices: 36-inch solid core wood, lever hardware
- Conference rooms: 36-inch with sidelight, lever hardware
- Restrooms: 36-inch solid core, kick plates
- Stairwell doors: 36-inch steel, fire-rated (90 min), self-closing

WINDOWS
- Curtain wall (south): floor-to-ceiling, 5 ft modules, low-e insulated glass
- Punched windows (east/west): 5 ft x 6 ft, aluminum frame, operable awning sash
- North ribbon: continuous 3 ft high x 60 ft total, fixed

ELECTRICAL
- Service: 480/277V 3-phase, 1200A main switchboard in basement
- Distribution: 208/120V panels on each floor via dry-type transformers
- Emergency: 150kW diesel generator on concrete pad at grade, ATS in electrical room
- Lighting: LED throughout, 2x4 troffers in offices, recessed downlights in lobby
- Emergency lighting: battery backup units at exits and stairwells

FIRE PROTECTION
- Wet sprinkler system throughout, 4-inch riser in fire riser room
- Standpipe in each stairwell
- Fire alarm: addressable system, smoke detectors in all spaces, pull stations at exits
- Server room: clean agent (FM-200) suppression system
