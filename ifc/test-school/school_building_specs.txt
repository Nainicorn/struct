EDUCATIONAL FACILITY SPECIFICATION
Project: Maple Ridge Elementary School — Single-Story K-5 School
Architect: Perkins & Will Education Studio
Date: August 2024

SITE & FOUNDATION
Building footprint: 180 ft x 140 ft (25,200 sq ft). Single-story except gymnasium with double-height ceiling. Reinforced concrete spread footings, 6-inch slab-on-grade with 15-mil vapor barrier. Perimeter frost wall to 48 inches. Building oriented east-west for optimal classroom daylighting.

STRUCTURAL SYSTEM
Steel frame with open web steel joists. Columns W10x49 at 30 ft bays. Standard ceiling height 12 ft in corridors and classrooms. Gymnasium clear span 60 ft x 80 ft, 24 ft clear height. Auditorium 40 ft x 60 ft, 18 ft clear. Roof: steel deck with built-up roofing.

CLASSROOM WING (6 Classrooms + 2 Labs)
- Classrooms 101-106: 30 ft x 25 ft each = 750 sq ft, VCT flooring
  - Each room: teacher desk area, 30 student stations, smartboard on north wall
  - Windows: 3 each, 4 ft x 5 ft, south-facing, motorized blinds
  - Doors: 36-inch solid core with vision lite, to corridor
  - Built-in cabinetry on west wall (12 ft), sink station
- Science Lab: 40 ft x 30 ft = 1,200 sq ft, epoxy flooring
  - 6 lab bench stations (5 ft x 3 ft each) with gas and water
  - Fume hood: 6 ft, located on north wall, dedicated exhaust
  - Emergency shower/eyewash station at entry
  - Chemical storage room: 6 ft x 8 ft, explosion-proof ventilation
- Computer Lab: 30 ft x 25 ft = 750 sq ft, carpet tile
  - 30 workstations, raised floor for cable management
  - Dedicated cooling: supplemental mini-split, 2-ton

MAIN CORRIDOR
- Width: 10 ft, total length approximately 160 ft
- VCT flooring, painted CMU walls
- Lockers on both sides in classroom wing (240 total, 12 in x 12 in x 36 in)
- 8 sets of double doors (36-inch each leaf) at wing transitions

GYMNASIUM

- Bleacher seating: retractable, 6 rows each side, capacity 200
- Ceiling height: 24 ft clear
- 6 basketball hoops (4 side, 2 main)
- Storage rooms: 2 each, 10 ft x 15 ft, rolling equipment doors
- Boys locker room: 15 ft x 20 ft, tile, 10 lockers, 3 showers
- Girls locker room: 15 ft x 20 ft, tile, 10 lockers, 3 showers
- Coach office: 10 ft x 10 ft

CAFETERIA

- Kitchen: 20 ft x 30 ft = 600 sq ft, quarry tile flooring
  - Walk-in cooler: 8 ft x 10 ft
  - Walk-in freezer: 8 ft x 8 ft
  - Cooking line: 20 ft (range, oven, fryer, steamer, kettle)

  - Dishwasher: conveyor type

- Stage wings: 10 ft x 20 ft each side
- Control booth: 10 ft x 8 ft, rear of auditorium

EXTERIOR
- Walls: brick veneer with CMU backup, R-19 cavity insulation
- Roof: fully-adhered TPO membrane, R-30 polyiso insulation
- Windows: aluminum frame, insulated low-e glass, all operable
- Main entry: aluminum storefront with vestibule (8 ft x 10 ft)
- Secure entry: video intercom, electric strike, buzzer system
- Canopy: 8 ft projection over main entry and bus loop

MECHANICAL
- AHU-1 (Classroom Wing): 12,000 CFM, serves all classrooms + labs + corridors
  - Energy recovery ventilator, MERV-14 filtration
  - VAV boxes at each classroom for individual temperature control
- AHU-2 (Gymnasium): 5,000 CFM, constant volume
- AHU-3 (Cafeteria/Kitchen): 3,500 CFM + kitchen hood makeup air
- Auditorium AHU: 4,000 CFM, CO2-based demand ventilation
- Boiler: 2,000 MBH condensing gas, hydronic heating loop
- Chiller: 80-ton air-cooled scroll, chilled water loop

ELECTRICAL
- Service: 480/277V 3-phase, 2000A
- Emergency generator: 200kW diesel, serves life safety and IT
- Lighting: LED throughout, daylight harvesting in classrooms
- PA/intercom system: IP-based, classroom speakers + hallway horns
- Security: 32 cameras, card access at all exterior doors
